Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit the Himalayas?

The best time is during the pre-monsoon season (March to May) and post-monsoon season (September to November) when the weather is clear and stable.

Do you offer guided tours?

I occasionally organize small group adventures. Contact me for upcoming trips and availability.

Can I share my mountain photos with you?

Absolutely! I love seeing other people's mountain adventures. Send them my way and I might feature them on the blog!
