My Story

Namaste! I'm Aadarsh Chhetri, a passionate mountain travel guide born and raised in the heart of Nepal. Growing up in the shadow of the mighty Himalayas, I developed an unbreakable bond with these majestic mountains from a young age.

My journey as a travel guide began over 10 years ago when I realized that sharing the beauty and culture of my homeland was not just a profession, but my true calling. Every trek, every mountain peak, and every cultural encounter has shaped me into the experienced guide I am today.
